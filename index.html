About Me

I am a Master’s graduate in Artificial Intelligence with a strong focus on bridging the gap between advanced Machine Learning research and practical industrial application. My expertise lies in building and deploying AI systems for manufacturing optimization, predictive maintenance, and computer vision.

During my Master’s thesis at Fraunhofer FFB, I engineered an AI-optimized battery manufacturing system using simulation and smart scheduling. I am pragmatic, detail-oriented, and driven to create scalable AI solutions that deliver real-world automation value.
